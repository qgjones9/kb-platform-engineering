# Platform One

Platform One (P1) is the U.S. military's software delivery engine.
As a pioneering Department of the Air Force organization, we provide open-source tools and enterprise solutions for teams to build, deploy, and secure better software at scale.

Since 2018, we've helped military organizations move faster and reduce the costs to create, integrate, and launch better software. We've streamlined the processes.
